Welcome to the Asteroid (NEO) Closest Approach Analysis App

Detailed Analysis of Near Earth Objects

Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) are asteroids, comets, and meteoroids with orbits that bring them close to Earth, typically within 1.3 astronomical units (AU) of the Sun. NEOs are monitored due to potential collision risks, and they offer valuable insights into the early solar system and planetary defense strategies.
